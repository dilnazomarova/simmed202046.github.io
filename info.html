ABOUT US

Affordable surgical simulators for students and young professionals!


Various types of general surgery simulators, where it is possible to work out the technique of incision, tying knots and suturing. These simulators are perfect for training and demonstrating practical skills in operative surgery, training in teamwork and assisting.


The design of the simulator allows you to practice on it in any place convenient for you. Any of the simulators will be a wonderful gift for a novice surgeon!
